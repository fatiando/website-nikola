These are the software projects that are part of Fatiando a Terra:

* `Fatiando a Terra <https://github.com/leouieda/fatiando>`_: 
  A `Python <http://python.org>`_ package for geophysical modeling and 
  inversion. Contains various algorithms and facilities for developing inverse
  problems.
* `Tesseroids <https://leouieda.github.com/tesseroids>`_: Commandline tools for
  forward modeling of gravitational fields in spherical coordinates.
